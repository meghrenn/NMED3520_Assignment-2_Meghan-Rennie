About

Meghan Rennie

Meghan Rennie has many fingers in many pies. Hopefully the following jumble of information coalesces into a sufficiently accurate and endearing portrait.

Creative Endeavours

Meghan Rennie is passionate about art, writing, music, DIY publishing, and graphic design. She works on a multitude of projects on a sporadically-professional basis. Most recently, she designed the layout for the art book Prairie Invasions by Emily Neufeld, published by the Southern Alberta Art Gallery.

Community Involvement

Meghan Rennie is an organizer, entrepreneur, leader, volunteer, researcher, advocate, or whatever else you want to call her. Her interests include environmentalism, non-profit management, conflict resolution, queer rights, indigenisation, and general prosociality. She served as the board president of the CKXU Radio Society, developed an Earth Day publication in partnership with the Southern Alberta Art Gallery, and worked as a coauthor on marketing research papers with Dr. Rhiannon Mesler.

Miscellaneous Details

Meghan Rennie is enamoured with people and pursuant of projects; she likes to braid ideas and compile data and formulate plans and make progress. She gets excited easily and tends to ramble. She collects buttons and pins, stickers, and patterned socks. She hopes you have a good day.
